# Privacy Policy for Actionforge

## Introduction
Welcome to Actionforge ("the App"). Actionforge is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. This privacy policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and sharing of your information.

## Information We Collect
The App collects the following types of information:
1. **GitHub Data**: Information related to your GitHub account, such as your username, public repositories, and commit information, as permitted by your GitHub permissions.
2. **Usage Data**: Information on how you interact with the App, including features used and time spent on the App.

## How We Use Your Information
The information we collect is used for the following purposes:
- To provide and improve the functionality of the App.
- To communicate with you about updates or changes to the App.
- To understand how users interact with the App, aiding in its improvement.

## Sharing of Your Information
We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to provide and improve the App, or as required by law.

## Data Security
We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from you to prevent loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes.

## Contact Us
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us Actionforge via [email](mailto:hello@actionforge.dev).
